Tomato Soup

The Coziest Tomato Soup You'll Eat This Fall!

This fall get ready to indulge in this warm and comforting tomato soup!.This recipe is simple and won't take too much of your time. Simple ingredients, but a bold and bright flavor. It'll be the perfect dish to bring to your next gathering!.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 large slice of onion
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine tomatoes, chicken broth, garlic cloves, and large slice of onion in a stockpot over medium heat. Bring to a boil, gently simmer for about 20 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and run the mixture through a food mill into a large bowl, or pan. Discard any stuff left over in the food mill.
  3. Melt butter over medium heat in the now empty stockpot. Stir in flour to make a roux by cooking, whisking contstantly, until mixture turns medium brown.
  4. Gradually whisk in a bit of the tomato mixture to prevent lumps from forming, then stir in the rest.
  5. Season with sugar and salt to taste.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
